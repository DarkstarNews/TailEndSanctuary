## 🎨 Design System

### Colors
- **Sage Dark**: `#5C6B54` (primary brand color, headers, buttons)
- **Sage**: `#8B9A7B` (accents, hover states)
- **Sage Light**: `#A8B89C` (backgrounds, subtle accents)
- **Cream**: `#F5F0E8` (page background)
- **Brown**: `#9B6B4B` (donate buttons, highlights)
- **Text**: `#3A3A3A` (body text)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), 600 weight
- **Body**: Inter (sans-serif), 400-600 weight
- **Font sizes**: Responsive with clamp() for fluid typography

- Navigation (sticky header)
- Hero variants (image, dark, simple)
- Buttons (primary, secondary, donate)
- Cards (dog cards, action cards, info cards)
- Sections (cream, white, sage backgrounds)
- Footer (4-column layout with logo, links, contact)
