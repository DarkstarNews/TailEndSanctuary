About Us

Giving senior dogs a peaceful, loving ending to their lives.

Our Story

At Tail End Sanctuary, our work is simple at its core: to give senior dogs a peaceful, loving ending to their lives.

This began with a pattern we could no longer ignore.

In our community, people kept identifying older stray dogs living on the streets on their own—dogs who were no longer young, no longer wanted, and often struggling just to get through each day.

They were easy to overlook, but impossible to forget once you truly saw them.

What started as concern turned into action. One dog led to another, and it became clear that these senior dogs needed something different—something beyond traditional rescue.

What We Do

We provide a safe, stable environment for senior stray dogs in the Lake Chapala area of Mexico.

Instead of placing them back into uncertainty, we focus on comfort, care, and a sense of peace.

  • • Daily care and nourishment
  • • Veterinary support and pain management
  • • A calm, secure environment
  • • Consistent human companionship

What Makes Us Different

We are not a traditional rescue.

Our focus is not on numbers or turnover, but on the quality of each dog's remaining time.

For many of the dogs who come to us, this is the first time they experience consistent kindness.
