Meet the Dogs

Every dog here has a story. Here are a few of them.

Teddy

Approximately 10 years old

Found wandering the streets alone and in pain. Teddy now has a warm bed, medical care, and people who make sure he is never alone again.

Luna

Approximately 12 years old

Luna was found sleeping under a market stall. She is partially blind but loves to lie in the sun and be petted by volunteers.

Canelo

Approximately 11 years old

Canelo lived on the same street corner for years. Now he has a soft bed and regular meals, and he greets every visitor with a slow, happy tail wag.

Paloma

Approximately 13 years old

The oldest dog at the sanctuary, Paloma moves slowly but always finds a sunny spot. She is calm, gentle, and deeply loved by everyone here.

More dogs coming soon. Each dog's profile will be updated as new residents arrive at the sanctuary.

Tail End Sanctuary

A safe haven for senior stray dogs in Lake Chapala, Mexico. Because every dog deserves comfort and love at the end.
