Meet the Dogs

Every dog here has a story.

Every dog here is loved.

These are the senior souls who now call Tail End Sanctuary home. Each one arrived carrying the weight of years on the street. Now, for the first time, they rest easy—because someone finally sees them.

From long-time companions to forgotten souls on the streets,

each dog deserves a peaceful, dignified life.

Get to know them. Share their stories. Help give them the best days they have left.
