Meet Teddy

Teddy was found wandering the streets at 10 years old—tired, alone, and in pain. He has cancer. But today, Teddy is safe. He has a warm bed, gentle medical care, and people who sit with him, comfort him, and make sure he is never alone again.

At Tail End Sanctuary, dogs like Teddy are not defined by how they suffered—but by how they are loved in the time they have left.
