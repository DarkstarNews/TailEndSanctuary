Get Involved

There are many ways to make a difference in the lives of senior dogs.

Volunteer

We welcome volunteers who can give their time to help care for the dogs. Whether it is spending an afternoon sitting with them, helping with feeding, or assisting with daily tasks, your presence matters.

Volunteer opportunities are available on-site in Lake Chapala. If you are local or visiting the area and would like to help, please reach out.

Advocate

Help us spread the word. Share our mission with your community, on social media, or with friends and family who care about animal welfare. Awareness is one of the most powerful forms of support.

Donate

Financial contributions go directly toward food, medical care, and maintaining a safe environment for the dogs. Every amount helps sustain the sanctuary and the dogs who depend on it.

Make a Donation

Supply Donations

We are always in need of dog food, blankets, beds, medications, and other supplies. If you would like to contribute supplies, contact us to learn what is most needed at this time.
